Khmer-Flavors

Khmer-Flavors serves breakfast, lunch and dinner, and also has private dining rooms for special requests. offers delicious homemade food, creative blend of authentic casual Khmer cuisine and Western classics, such as Amok Trey, Burger, and Lok Lak fusing the distinctive light flavour of the traditional Amok and blended in a hearty burger. No matter how often you dine at Khmer-Flavors, you will always find something delicious to try with the restaurant's specialties.
